About

Ever been stuck in traffic around Downtown Seattle as thousands of sports fans converge on Safeco or CenturyLink Field? Is There a Game Today? was created to help commuters in the Seattle area plan their day around professional sports events. Currently this web app keeps track of sports games played in SODO - namely Mariners, Seahawks, and Sounders games.
